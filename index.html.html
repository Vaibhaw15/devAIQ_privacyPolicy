1. Introduction

Welcome to DevAIQ — a developer quiz app designed to help users test and improve their knowledge of modern development technologies.

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information (via Firebase Authentication)

  • Name
  • Email address

b. Quiz & Progress Data

  • Quiz categories and levels you’ve attempted
  • Scores, attempts, and history
  • Preferences and bookmarks (if applicable)

c. Device & Usage Info

  • Device type, OS version
  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • User interaction data (Firebase Analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users and manage sessions
  • Store quiz results and user progress
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze engagement and fix issues

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is securely stored on Google Firebase servers using encryption and industry-standard practices.

  • HTTPS for all communications
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access control and authentication

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share limited data only:

  • With Firebase to provide essential services
  • When required by law
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

6. Children’s Privacy

DevAIQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe we’ve unintentionally collected such data.
